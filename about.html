Designing Architecture That Inspires

Elyon is an international architecture, real estate development, and land investment company committed to shaping meaningful spaces and sustainable communities across Africa.

Our architectural practice focuses on innovative, functional, and context-driven design, delivering projects that balance aesthetics, efficiency, and long-term value. From residential homes and commercial buildings to institutional and large-scale developments, we approach every project with a deep commitment to excellence and detail.

The Architect

Madjilem Raison

"Architecture is not just about building walls; it's about crafting experiences and shaping the future of how we live."

Madjilem Raison is an architect and entrepreneur originally from Chad and currently based in Kigali, Rwanda. Trained in architecture in Togo, he has developed a strong passion for designing spaces that combine functionality, cultural identity, and long-term value for communities.

He is the founder and CEO of Elyon, an architecture and real estate company dedicated to delivering innovative architectural solutions across Africa. Through his work, Madjilem focuses not only on architectural design but also on land development, real estate opportunities, and large-scale projects that contribute to sustainable urban growth.

Over the years, he has contributed to various architectural and development projects in Rwanda, ranging from residential homes and interior design to institutional and large-scale developments. His approach combines creativity, strategic thinking, and a deep understanding of the social and economic context of African cities.
